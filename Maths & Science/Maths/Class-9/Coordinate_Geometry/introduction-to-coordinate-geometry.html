üìΩÔ∏è Story Time: The Cinema Ticket

Imagine karo tum cinema hall mein movie dekhne gaye ho. Tumhare ticket par likha hai:

Seat: H-12

Iska matlab kya hai?

  • H: Row number (Kaunsi line hai).
  • 12: Us row mein kaunsi seat hai.

Agar sirf "Row H" pata ho, toh tum confuse ho jaoge ki kaunsi seat par baithna hai. Agar sirf "Seat 12" pata ho, toh tumhe pata nahi chalega ki kaunsi line mein jaana hai. Tumhe Dono information chahiye ek specific seat par pahunchne ke liye.

Bas yahi Coordinate Geometry ka basic idea hai: Kisi point ko locate karne ke liye humein Do (2) pieces of information chahiye hoti hain.

üë¥ History: The Fly on the Ceiling

Ye idea sabse pehle 17th Century mein ek French mathematician Ren√© Descartes ko aaya tha.

Ek din wo apne bed par leta hua tha aur chhat (ceiling) par ek makkhi (fly) ko dekh raha tha. Usne socha, "Agar mujhe is makkhi ki exact position kisi ko batani ho, toh main kaise bataunga?"

Usne realize kiya ki wo ceiling ke do adjacent walls (kone wali deewarein) se distance naap kar position bata sakta hai. Example: "Makkhi left wall se 2 meter dur hai aur bottom wall se 3 meter dur hai."

Is system ko uske naam par Cartesian System kaha jata hai.
