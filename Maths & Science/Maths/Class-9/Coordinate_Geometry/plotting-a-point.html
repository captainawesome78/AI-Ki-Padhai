Point plot karna ek simple game jaisa hai. Imagine karo tum Origin (0,0) par khade ho.

üö∂ The "Walk from Home" Method

Step 1: Start at Origin (Yahan se shuru karo).

Step 2: Look at x-coordinate (Horizontal Move)

  • Agar Positive (+) hai ‚Üí Move RIGHT üëâ
  • Agar Negative (-) hai ‚Üí Move LEFT üëà
  • Agar Zero (0) hai ‚Üí Wahi ruko (No Left/Right move).

Step 3: Look at y-coordinate (Vertical Move)

  • Agar Positive (+) hai ‚Üí Move UP üëÜ
  • Agar Negative (-) hai ‚Üí Move DOWN üëá
  • Agar Zero (0) hai ‚Üí Wahi ruko (No Up/Down move).

Step 4: Mark the Point

Jahan tum finally pahuncho, wahi tumhara point hai!

üìö Examples of Plotting

1. Quadrant I Example: (3, 4)

x = +3 üëâ Move 3 units RIGHT
y = +4 üëÜ Then, Move 4 units UP

Ye point Quadrant I mein hoga.

2. Quadrant II Example: (-2, 5)

x = -2 üëà Move 2 units LEFT
y = +5 üëÜ Then, Move 5 units UP

Ye point Quadrant II mein hoga.

3. Quadrant III Example: (-5, -3)

x = -5 üëà Move 5 units LEFT
y = -3 üëá Then, Move 3 units DOWN

Ye point Quadrant III mein hoga.

4. Quadrant IV Example: (6, -2)

x = +6 üëâ Move 6 units RIGHT
y = -2 üëá Then, Move 2 units DOWN

Ye point Quadrant IV mein hoga.

üõë Points on the Axes (Zero Wale Points)

Students sabse zyada galti tab karte hain jab coordinates mein 0 aajata hai. Chalo isse clear karte hain.

Case A: Point on X-Axis (4, 0)

x = +4 üëâ Move 4 units RIGHT
y = 0 üõë Stop! (No Up/Down move)

Ye point directly X-Axis line ke upar hoga.

Case B: Point on Y-Axis (0, -3)

x = 0 üõë Stop! (Start from Origin, don't go Left/Right)
y = -3 üëá Move 3 units DOWN on the line itself

Ye point directly Y-Axis line ke upar hoga.

Recap:

  • Coordinate Geometry locations find karta hai.
