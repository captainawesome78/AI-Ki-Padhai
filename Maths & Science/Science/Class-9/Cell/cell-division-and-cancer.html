Ek Se Anek 🌱

Aap kabhi ek chhote se cell the! Maa ke pet mein aapki shuruaat sirf ek single cell se hui thi. Aaj aapke body mein takreeban 37,000,000,000,000 (37 trillion) cells hain. Ye jaadu kaise hua? Cell Division ke through — ek cell do banta hai, do se chaar, chaar se aath... aur ye silsila chalta rehta hai.

📌 Cell Theory ka teesra niyam yaad hai? "Har cell doosre cell se banta hai." Cell division hi wo process hai jisse naye cells bante hain.

❓ Cells Divide Kyun Karte Hain?

  • Growth (badhna): Bachche se bade banne ke liye zyada cells chahiye.
  • Repair (marammat): Chot lagne par nayi cells purani kharab cells ki jagah leti hain (zakhm bharna).
  • Reproduction (prajanan): Naye jeev banane ke liye special cells (gametes) banti hain.
🩹 Kya Aap Jaante Hain?

Jab aapko chot lagti hai aur khaal (skin) apne aap theek ho jaati hai — ye cell division ka kamaal hai! Nayi skin cells banti hain aur ghaav (wound) bhar deti hain. Aapke baal aur nakhun bhi cell division ki wajah se badhte hain.

➗ Do Types Of Cell Division

1. Mitosis — "Copy-Paste" wali Division

Isme ek cell do bilkul same (identical) cells banata hai. Dono naye cells mein utne hi chromosomes hote hain jitne parent cell mein the. Ye body ke normal cells (skin, muscle, etc.) ki growth aur repair ke liye hoti hai.

✔ Mitosis ka result:

1 parent cell → 2 identical daughter cells (same DNA). Jaise ek document ki photocopy nikalna.

2. Meiosis — "Aadha-Aadha" wali Division

Ye sirf special reproductive cells (gametes — sperm aur egg) banane ke liye hoti hai. Isme ek cell chaar cells banata hai, aur har naye cell mein chromosomes aadhe (half) ho jaate hain. Ye zaroori hai taaki jab sperm + egg milein toh baby mein poore (full) chromosomes ho.

🚨 Jab Division Control Se Bahar Ho Jaaye — Cancer

Normally, cell division ek control mein hoti hai — jitni zaroorat, utni cells banti hain, phir ruk jaati hain. Lekin kabhi-kabhi ek cell "control" kho deta hai aur bina ruke baar-baar divide karta rehta hai. Ye cells ek gaanth (tumour) bana leti hain. Isi ko Cancer kehte hain.

🚗 Analogy: Bina Brake Wali Gaadi

Socho ek gaadi jiske brake fail ho gaye. Wo rukti hi nahi, aage badhti rehti hai aur nuksaan karti hai. Cancer cells bhi aisi hi hain — inke "brake" (jo division ko rokte hain) kaam nahi karte, isliye ye bina control ke badhti rehti hain.

🛡️ Bachaav (Prevention): Tambaku (tobacco), gutkha aur cigarette cancer ke sabse bade kaaran hain. Inse door rehna, healthy khana khana, aur dhoop (sun) mein zyada der na jhulasna cancer ka risk kam karta hai. Cancer ka jaldi pata chale toh iska ilaaj (surgery, chemotherapy, radiation) mumkin hai.

📌 Ye ek sensitive topic hai. Cancer ke baare mein padhna important hai taaki hum bachaav kar sakein. Agar aapke ghar mein koi is se guzar raha hai, toh doctor se baat karna sabse accha kadam hai.

🇮🇳 Bharat Ka Yogdaan (India's Contribution)

🔬 Professor Arun Kumar Sharma

Bharat ke mahaan cell biologist. Unhone chromosomes ko study karne ke naye tareeke (techniques) banaye, jinse aaj bhi duniya bhar mein cells aur genes ki research hoti hai. Unka kaam plant chromosomes par bahut famous hai.

🧬 Naya Zamaana: Cell Biology Ki Taraqqi

Aaj scientists stem cells se kharab organs theek karne ki koshish kar rahe hain, aur Leigh Syndrome jaisi bimariyon ko samajh rahe hain jo mitochondria ke kharab hone se hoti hain. Ek din shayad hum "artificial cell" bhi bana payenge!
