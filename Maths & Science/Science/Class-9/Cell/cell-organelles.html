Cell = Ek Chhota Sheher 🏙️

Ab hum cell ke andar chalte hain! Cell ke andar chhote-chhote "ang" hote hain jinhe Organelles kehte hain ("organelle" = "chhota organ"). Jaise ek sheher mein power house, factory, office, aur safai wale hote hain — waise hi cell mein har organelle ka apna kaam hai.

🏙️ The City Analogy

Socho cell ek sheher hai:
▸ Nucleus = Mayor ka office (saare orders yahin se nikalte hain)
▸ Mitochondria = Power house (bijli/energy banata hai)
▸ Ribosomes = Chhoti factories (protein banati hain)
▸ ER = Roads / transport network
▸ Golgi Body = Post office / courier (packing & delivery)
▸ Lysosome = Safai gaadi / kachra saaf karne wale

🗺️ Poora Map — Ek Nazar Mein

Ek typical cell aur uske main organelles. Neeche har ek ka kaam detail mein.

🔎 Ek-Ek Karke Samjho

Boss / Control Room

🧠 1. Nucleus

Cell ka sabse important part. Isme DNA (poori "instruction book") rehti hai. Nucleus hi decide karta hai ki cell kya kare, kab divide ho, kaunsa protein bane. Iske andar ek chhota gol part nucleolus hota hai jo ribosomes banata hai. Nucleus ke bahar nuclear membrane hoti hai jisme chhote pores (chhed) hote hain.

Power House

🔋 2. Mitochondria

Ise cell ka "Power House" kehte hain. Ye khaane (glucose) ko todkar ATP (energy ka "currency note") banata hai — respiration ke through. Jitna zyada kaam wala cell (jaise muscle cell), utne zyada mitochondria. Khaas baat: iska apna DNA hota hai!

Protein Factory

🏭 3. Ribosomes

Cell ke sabse chhote organelle. Inka ek hi kaam — protein banana. Ye ya toh cytoplasm mein khule tairte hain, ya ER ki surface par chipke hote hain. Protein humari body ke "building blocks" hain (baal, nakhun, muscles sab protein se bane).

Transport & Roads

🛣️ 4. Endoplasmic Reticulum (ER)

Cell ke andar naali-numa (tube-like) network — ye "roads" ki tarah cheezein ek jagah se doosri jagah pahunchata hai. Do types:

  • Rough ER (RER) — ispe ribosomes chipke hote hain (isliye "rough"), protein transport karta hai.
  • Smooth ER (SER) — bina ribosomes, fats/lipids banata hai aur poison saaf karta hai (detoxification).
Post Office / Courier

📦 5. Golgi Apparatus (Golgi Body)

Ye ER se cheezein leta hai, unhe pack karta hai, label lagata hai, aur sahi address par deliver karta hai. Isiliye ise cell ka "post office" ya "packaging & dispatch centre" kehte hain. Ise Camillo Golgi ne khoja tha.

Safai / Cleaner

🧹 6. Lysosomes

Ye "kachra saaf karne wale" hain. Inme powerful digestive enzymes hote hain jo purane/kharab parts aur bahar se aaye germs ko tod-tod kar khatam karte hain. Jab cell mar jaata hai, lysosome khud phatta hai aur cell ko digest kar deta hai — isliye ise "Suicide Bag" bhi kehte hain!

Storage Tank

💧 7. Vacuoles

Storage room — paani, food, aur waste store karte hain. Plant cell mein ek bada central vacuole hota hai jo cell ko shape aur "tightness" (turgidity) deta hai.

Solar Kitchen (sirf plants)

🌞 8. Plastids / Chloroplast

Sirf plant cells mein. Chloroplast (green plastid) mein chlorophyll hota hai jo sunlight se khana banata hai (photosynthesis). Doosre plastids fruits/flowers ko rang dete hain (chromoplast) ya food store karte hain (leucoplast).
