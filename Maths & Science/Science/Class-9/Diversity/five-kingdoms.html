Duniya Ke 5 "Rajya" (Kingdoms) 👑

Saal 1969 mein R.H. Whittaker ne saare jeevon ko 5 bade groups (kingdoms) mein baanta. Ye baantne ke liye unhone dekha: cell type (nucleus hai ya nahi), cell number (ek ya bahut), aur nutrition (khaana khud banata hai ya nahi).

Whittaker ke 5 kingdoms - simple (Monera) se complex (Animalia) tak.

📊 Ek Nazar Mein - Comparison Table

KingdomCell typeCellsNutritionExample
MoneraProkaryotic (no nucleus)UnicellularAuto ya heteroBacteria, blue-green algae
ProtistaEukaryoticUnicellularAuto ya heteroAmoeba, Paramecium, Euglena
FungiEukaryoticMostly multicellularHeterotroph (saprophyte)Mushroom, yeast, mould
PlantaeEukaryoticMulticellularAutotroph (photosynthesis)Ped, paudhe, algae
AnimaliaEukaryoticMulticellularHeterotrophInsaan, machhli, keede

📌 Sabse bada difference: Monera ke paas nucleus NAHI hota (prokaryotic), baaki 4 kingdoms ke paas nucleus hota hai (eukaryotic). Aur Plantae apna khaana khud banati hai, jabki Fungi & Animalia doosron par nirbhar (dependent) hain.

🍽️ Nutrition - Ek Zaroori Baat

  • Autotrophs (khud khaana banane wale) - Plants (sunlight se photosynthesis).
  • Heterotrophs (doosron se khaana lene wale) - Animals (khaate hain), Fungi (saprophyte - marte/sadte padarth se khaana soakte hain).
🍄 Fungi Plant Nahi Hai!

Bahut log samajhte hain mushroom ek plant hai - galat! Fungi ke paas chlorophyll nahi hota, wo apna khaana khud nahi bana sakte. Wo marte/sadte cheezon se khaana lete hain, isliye unka apna alag kingdom hai.
