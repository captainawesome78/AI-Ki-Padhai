🍄 Kingdom Fungi

Fungi na plant hain na animal - inka apna alag kingdom hai. Ye eukaryotic, zyada-tar multicellular (yeast ek exception - unicellular), aur heterotroph hote hain. Inke paas chlorophyll nahi hota, isliye ye apna khaana khud nahi bana sakte.

Khaas Baatein

Fungi ki pehchaan

  • Saprophytic nutrition - marte/sadte cheezon se khaana soakte hain (decomposers).
  • Cell wall chitin ki hoti hai (cellulose nahi).
  • Body dhaagon (hyphae) se banti hai, jinke jaal ko mycelium kehte hain.
  • Kuch parasitic (doosron par palte) aur kuch symbiotic (saath rehkar dono ko faayda) hote hain.

Examples: Mushroom (khumbi), Yeast (bread/idli phulaane wala), Rhizopus (bread mould), Penicillium (jisse antibiotic penicillin banti hai).

💊 Fungus Se Dawa

Duniya ki pehli antibiotic - Penicillin - ek fungus (Penicillium) se bani thi! Isne crores logon ki jaan bachaayi. Aur roti/idli/dosa phulaane mein yeast (fungus) hi kaam aata hai. Fungi hamesha bure nahi hote!

🌿 Lichens - Ek Team

Lichen ek fungus aur ek algae ki dosti (symbiosis) hai - algae khaana banata hai, fungus paani aur surksha deta hai. Ye pathron (rocks) par bhi ug sakte hain aur pollution ke "indicators" hote hain.

🌳 Kingdom Plantae

Ye saare ped, paudhe aur algae ka kingdom hai. Khaas baatein: eukaryotic, multicellular, autotroph (chlorophyll se khud khaana banate hain - photosynthesis), aur cell wall cellulose ki hoti hai.

Plant kingdom ko unke body design ke hisaab se groups mein baanta gaya hai - simple se complex tak:

🟢 Thallophyta (Algae)

Sabse simple plants. Body ka koi proper root, stem, ya leaf nahi (isse "thallus" kehte hain). Paani mein rehte hain. Example: Spirogyra, Ulva, Chara.

🌿 Bryophyta (Moss)

"Plant kingdom ke amphibians" - naam ke plants jo naam (moist) jagah par ugte hain. Simple body, koi proper vascular tissue nahi. Example: Moss, Marchantia, Funaria.

🌱 Pteridophyta (Ferns)

Pehle plants jinme proper root, stem, leaf AUR vascular tissue (xylem/phloem) hota hai. Seeds nahi bante. Example: Fern (paanri), Marsilea, Horsetail.

🌲 Gymnosperms

Naked seeds (beej khule, kisi fruit ke andar nahi). Usually cones (jaise pine cone) hote hain, hare saal bhar. Example: Pine, Cycas, Deodar.

🌺 Angiosperms (Flowering Plants)

Phool wale plants. Seeds fruit ke andar band (covered). Sabse advanced aur sabse zyada. Do type:

  • Monocots - seed mein 1 cotyledon, parallel leaf-veins. Example: makka (maize), gehun, ghaas.
  • Dicots - seed mein 2 cotyledons, net-like leaf-veins. Example: chana (gram), matar, aam.
