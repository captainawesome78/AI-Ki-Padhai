Sabse Purane, Sabse Simple 🕰️

Monera aur Protista - ye dono kingdoms sabse simple jeevon ke hain, aur dono zyada-tar unicellular (ek cell wale) hote hain. Farak sirf itna: Monera ke paas nucleus NAHI hota (prokaryotic), aur Protista ke paas hota hai (eukaryotic).

🦠 Kingdom Monera

Ye Earth ke sabse purane jeev hain! Inki khaas baatein:

  • Prokaryotic - koi proper nucleus ya membrane-wale organelles nahi.
  • Hamesha unicellular.
  • Kuch ke paas cell wall hoti hai.
  • Nutrition auto (khud banane wale) ya hetero (doosron se) dono ho sakti hai.
Bacteria alag-alag shapes ke hote hain - gol, rod, aur spiral.

Examples: Bacteria, Cyanobacteria (Blue-green algae jaise Nostoc, Anabaena), Mycoplasma (sabse chhota cell).

🥛 Dost aur Dushman Dono

Kuch bacteria hamare dost hain - dahi (curd) banate hain, khaana pachaate hain, mitti mein nitrogen fix karte hain. Kuch dushman hain - TB, typhoid jaisi bimariyan phailate hain. Isliye safai zaroori hai!

🌀 Kingdom Protista

Ye "bridge" kingdom hai - inme kuch plant jaise, kuch animal jaise features hote hain. Khaas baatein:

  • Eukaryotic - proper nucleus hota hai.
  • Zyada-tar unicellular.
  • Kai mein chalne ke liye special parts - pseudopodia, cilia, ya flagella.
Protista ke jeev alag-alag tareekon se chalte hain.

Examples: Amoeba (pseudopodia se chalta), Paramecium (cilia se), Euglena (flagellum se; ye khaana khud bhi banata hai aur doosron se bhi leta hai!), diatoms.
