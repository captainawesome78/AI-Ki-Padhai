Momentum Kabhi "Gaayab" Nahi Hota 💫

Newton ke teesre niyam (action-reaction) se ek bahut powerful niyam nikalta hai. Jab do cheezein aapas mein takraati hain (ya ek doosre ko dhakelti hain), toh unka kul momentum reaction se pehle aur baad mein barabar rehta hai - bilkul waise jaise mass conserve hota hai chemistry mein.

📌 Law of Conservation of Momentum: Jab do (ya zyada) objects sirf aapas mein force lagate hain (koi bahari force na ho), toh unka total momentum constant rehta hai.

Reaction se pehle ka total momentum = reaction ke baad ka total momentum

📐 Do objects ke liye:
m₁u₁ + m₂u₂ = m₁v₁ + m₂v₂
(u = pehle ki velocity, v = baad ki velocity)

🎱 Solved Examples

✔ Example 1 - Do gend ki takkar:

Ek 2 kg ki gend 5 m/s se chalkar ek rukti hui 3 kg ki gend se takraati hai. Takkar ke baad 2 kg wali gend ruk jaati hai. 3 kg wali gend ki velocity?

Pehle: total p = (2 × 5) + (3 × 0) = 10 kg·m/s
Baad: total p = (2 × 0) + (3 × v) = 3v
Conservation se: 10 = 3v → v = 3.33 m/s

✔ Example 2 - Gun ka recoil:

Ek 4 kg ki gun se 0.02 kg ki goli 100 m/s se nikalti hai. Gun kis velocity se peeche jaayegi (recoil)?

Pehle: dono ruke the, total p = 0
Baad: goli ka p + gun ka p = 0
(0.02 × 100) + (4 × v) = 0
2 + 4v = 0 → v = −0.5 m/s
Minus sign matlab gun goli ke ulti direction mein 0.5 m/s se peeche jaati hai (recoil).

✔ Example 3 - Do cart jud jaate hain:

Ek 1 kg ka cart 6 m/s se chalkar ek rukti hui 2 kg ke cart se takraakar chipak jaata hai. Dono ki combined velocity?

Pehle: (1 × 6) + (2 × 0) = 6 kg·m/s
Baad: dono saath (1 + 2 = 3 kg) velocity v se: 3 × v
6 = 3v → v = 2 m/s

🚀 Rocket Aur Recoil - Conservation Ka Kamaal

🚀 Rocket Ki Asli Science

Rocket ka poora funda conservation of momentum par hai! Shuruaat mein rocket ruka hai (total momentum = 0). Jab wo gas neeche phenkti hai (gas ka momentum neeche), toh momentum conserve rakhne ke liye rocket upar chala jaata hai (rocket ka momentum upar). Dono barabar aur ulte - total abhi bhi 0!

🎈 Balloon Rocket Ghar Par

Ek dhaage (string) par straw laga do, straw mein dhaaga dalo, phir ek bhare balloon ko straw se chipka kar chhod do - balloon dhaage par tez chala jaayega! Hawa peeche, balloon aage - conservation of momentum ka mazedaar experiment.
