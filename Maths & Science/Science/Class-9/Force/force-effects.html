Hello Students! 👋

Ek gend ko thokar maaro - wo chal padti hai. Chalti gend ko haath se roko - wo ruk jaati hai. Rubber ko kheencho - wo lamba ho jaata hai. In sabme ek common cheez hai - aapne ek "dhakka" ya "kheench" lagayi. Isi dhakke ya kheench ko physics mein Force (bal) kehte hain.

📌 Force = ek push (dhakka) ya pull (kheench) jo kisi object par lagti hai. Ye khud dikhti nahi, lekin iske effects (asar) dikhte hain. SI unit: Newton (N).

Force ka concept itna important hai ki Isaac Newton ne iske teen niyam banaye jo poore physics ki neev hain - jinhe hum is chapter mein padhenge. Lekin pehle samjhein ki force kya-kya kar sakti hai.

🎯 Force Ke 5 Effects (Asar)

Ek force kisi object par ye paanch kaam kar sakti hai:

  1. Ruki cheez ko chala sakti hai: ruki hui football ko kick karo, wo chal padegi.
  2. Chalti cheez ko rok sakti hai: catch karke gend ko rok lena.
  3. Speed badal sakti hai: cycle chalate waqt zyada zor lagao, tez ho jaayegi.
  4. Direction badal sakti hai: football ko header maarna - wo mud jaati hai.
  5. Shape/size badal sakti hai: rubber ko kheenchna, aate ko dabana (chapati banana), spring dabana.

📌 Pehle 4 effects motion se jude hain (state of motion badalti hai), aur 5th effect shape se juda hai. Yaad rakho: force ya toh gati (motion) badalti hai ya aakaar (shape).

🏏 Cricket Mein Force Ke Kamaal

Ek cricket match mein aap force ke saare effects dekh sakte ho: bowler gend ko chalata hai, batsman use maarke direction aur speed badal deta hai, fielder use rokta hai, aur tez shot se gend thodi picak (deform) bhi jaati hai!

⚖️ Balanced vs Unbalanced Forces

Ek object par aksar ek se zyada forces ek saath lagti hain. Inka mila-jula asar (net force) hi decide karta hai ki object kya karega. Do situations hoti hain:

Net force = 0

Balanced Forces

Jab object par lagne wali saari forces ek doosre ko cancel kar dein (net force = 0). Aise mein object ki gati (state of motion) nahi badalti - jo ruka hai wo ruka rahega, jo chal raha hai wo usi speed aur direction se chalta rahega. Lekin object ka shape badal sakta hai.

Example: Rassa-kashi (tug of war) mein jab dono team barabar zor lagayein - rassa nahi hilta (par tan jaata hai). Mez par rakhi kitaab - gravity neeche kheenchti hai, mez upar dhakelti hai, dono barabar, kitaab ruki rehti hai.

Net force ≠ 0

Unbalanced Forces

Jab forces ek doosre ko cancel na karein (net force koi ek value ho). Aise mein object ki gati badal jaati hai - wo chalne lagta hai, ya tez/dheema hota hai, ya mud jaata hai. Yaani unbalanced force hi motion ka asli kaaran hai!

Example: Tug of war mein jab ek team zyada zor lagaye - rassa us taraf khisak jaata hai. Football ko kick karna - net force gend ko aage bhejti hai.

Balanced = forces cancel, object nahi hilta. Unbalanced = net force object ko hilata hai.

🤝 Friction - Ek Chhupi Hui Force

Ek gend ko farsh par ludhkaao - wo thodi der baad apne aap ruk kyun jaati hai? Kyunki ek chhupi hui force use rok rahi hai - Friction (gharshan). Jab do surfaces ek doosre par ragad (rub) khaati hain, tab friction lagti hai jo motion ka virodh (oppose) karti hai.

📌 Friction hamesha motion ke ulti direction mein lagti hai. Isi wajah se cheezein apne aap ruk jaati hain - warna ek baar chali cheez hamesha chalti rehti!

🧊 Friction Dost Bhi, Dushman Bhi

Friction dost hai - iske bina hum chal hi nahi paate (barf/ice par phisalte ho na?), gaadi ke brake kaam nahi karte, aur pen se likh nahi paate. Lekin ye dushman bhi hai - machine ke parts ghiste (wear out) hain aur energy waste hoti hai (isi liye tel/oil daalte hain). Ise hum aage detail mein padhenge, abhi bas itna yaad rakho ki friction ek force hai jo motion rokti hai.
