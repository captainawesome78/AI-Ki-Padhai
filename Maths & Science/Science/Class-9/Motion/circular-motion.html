Gol-Gol Ghoomna 🎡

Ab tak humne seedhi line wali motion (linear motion) dekhi. Lekin duniya mein bahut si cheezein gol raaste par chalti hain - ghadi ki suiyan, pankha, giant wheel, chaand prithvi ke charon taraf, aur satellites. Jab koi object ek gol raaste (circular path) par constant speed se chalta hai, use Uniform Circular Motion kehte hain.

📌 Uniform Circular Motion = gol raaste par ek hi (constant) speed se chalna.

🤯 Ye "Accelerated Motion" Kyun Hai?

Ye is lesson ka sabse important aur sabse chaunkane wala point hai. Object ki speed toh constant hai, phir bhi hum kehte hain ki ye accelerated motion hai. Kaise?

Yaad karo: velocity ek vector hai - usme magnitude (speed) aur direction dono hote hain. Gol raaste par chalte waqt object ki direction har pal badal rahi hoti hai. Direction badli, matlab velocity badli. Aur velocity badalna hi acceleration hai!

Velocity hamesha tangent (chhoone wali line) ke saath hoti hai; acceleration centre ki taraf.

📌 Circular motion mein velocity ki direction hamesha tangent (circle ko us point par chhoone wali seedhi line) ke saath hoti hai. Aur acceleration hamesha centre ki taraf hoti hai - ise centripetal acceleration kehte hain.

🪢 "Dori Kaato" Wala Experiment

🪢 Patthar aur Dori

Ek dori mein patthar baandhkar use gol-gol ghumao. Ab agar aap dori chhod do, toh patthar kis taraf jaayega? Bahut log sochte hain wo bahar ki taraf (radially) udega - lekin nahi!

Patthar us waqt jis direction mein ja raha tha, usi tangent ki seedhi line mein udega. Ye saabit karta hai ki circular motion mein velocity tangential hoti hai, aur dori uspar centre ki taraf force lagakar use gol raaste par rakhti thi.

🏏 Hammer Throw aur Cricket

Olympic ke "hammer throw" mein khiladi hammer ko gol ghumata hai aur phir chhod deta hai - hammer tangent ki direction mein door udta hai. Isi tarah, geeli chhatri (umbrella) ghumane par paani ki boondein tangent mein udti hain!

📐 Speed Nikalna

Ek poore chakkar mein object circle ki parimiti (circumference) jitni doori tay karta hai:

📐 Speed (v) = 2πr ÷ t
jahan r = circle ka radius, t = ek poora chakkar lagane ka samay

✔ Example 1:

Ek athlete 100 m radius ke gol track ka ek chakkar 62.8 second mein pura karta hai. Speed?
v = 2πr ÷ t = (2 × 3.14 × 100) ÷ 62.8 = 628 ÷ 62.8 = 10 m/s

✔ Example 2:

Ek ladka 7 m radius ke circle par 22 second mein ek chakkar lagata hai. Speed? (π = 22/7)
v = 2 × (22/7) × 7 ÷ 22 = 44 ÷ 22 = 2 m/s

🚀 ISRO aur Satellites

Bharat ke ISRO dwara chhode gaye satellites (jaise INSAT, Chandrayaan) prithvi ya chaand ke charon taraf lagbhag uniform circular motion mein ghoomte hain. Inhe apne orbit mein bane rehne ke liye centripetal force chahiye, jo gravity deti hai. Isi science se hamare TV signals, mausam ki jaankari (weather forecast) aur GPS chalte hain!
