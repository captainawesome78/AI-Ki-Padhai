Ek Tasveer, Hazaar Numbers 📊

Motion ko samjhne ka sabse achha tareeka hai graph. Ek nazar mein graph bata deta hai ki object ruka hua hai, uniform speed se chal raha hai, ya accelerate kar raha hai. Class 9 mein hum do graphs padhte hain: Distance-Time (s-t) aur Velocity-Time (v-t).

📌 Graph mein hamesha: Time x-axis (neeche) par, aur distance ya velocity y-axis (khada) par hoti hai.

📉 Distance-Time (s-t) Graph

📌 s-t graph ka SLOPE (dhalaan) = SPEED
Jitni tez dhalaan, utni zyada speed. Flat line = ruka hua.

Distance-time graph: flat = rest, seedhi line = uniform, curve = accelerated.
Graph ka shapeKya matlab
Horizontal (flat) lineObject rest mein hai (distance nahi badal rahi)
Seedhi tirchi (slanted) lineUniform motion - constant speed
Jitni zyada tirchi (steep)Utni zyada speed
Curve (upar mudta hua)Accelerated motion - speed badh rahi hai

✔ Example 1 - Slope se speed nikalna:

Ek s-t graph mein object 4 second mein 40 metre chalta hai (seedhi line).
Speed = slope = distance ÷ time = 40 ÷ 4 = 10 m/s

📈 Velocity-Time (v-t) Graph

Ye graph aur bhi zyada powerful hai, kyunki isse hum do cheezein nikaal sakte hain:

📌 v-t graph ka SLOPE = ACCELERATION
📌 v-t graph ke neeche ka AREA = DISTANCE (displacement)
Ye do baatein exam mein bahut poochi jaati hain!

v-t graph: flat = uniform velocity, upar jaati line = acceleration, neeche jaati = retardation. Shaded area = distance.

📐 Area Se Distance Nikalna - Solved Examples

✔ Example 2 - Uniform velocity (rectangle area):

Ek gaadi 10 m/s ki constant velocity se 5 second chalti hai. v-t graph ek rectangle banega.
Distance = area = lambai × chaudai = velocity × time
= 10 × 5 = 50 m

✔ Example 3 - Uniform acceleration (triangle area):

Ek gaadi rest (0) se chalkar 4 second mein 20 m/s ki velocity paati hai. Distance kitni chali?
Graph ek triangle banega.
Distance = area of triangle = ½ × base × height
= ½ × 4 × 20 = 40 m

✔ Example 4 - Slope se acceleration:

Usi graph mein acceleration nikalo.
Acceleration = slope = (v − u) ÷ t = (20 − 0) ÷ 4 = 5 m/s²

✔ Example 5 - Trapezium (mila-jula case):

Ek gaadi 10 m/s se chalkar 6 second mein 30 m/s ho jaati hai. Distance?
Shape ek trapezium hai.
Area = ½ × (dono parallel sides ka jod) × height = ½ × (10 + 30) × 6
= ½ × 40 × 6 = 120 m
