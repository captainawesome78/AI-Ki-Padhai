Pichle lesson mein humne dekha ki object kitna chala. Ab dekhte hain wo kitni tezi se chala - yaani Speed aur Velocity. Ye dono ek jaise lagte hain lekin inme ek bahut zaroori farak hai.

⏱️ Uniform aur Non-Uniform Motion

📌 Uniform motion = object barabar samay mein barabar doori tay kare.
Non-uniform motion = barabar samay mein alag-alag doori tay kare.

TimeUniform motion (distance)Non-uniform motion (distance)
1st second10 m5 m
2nd second10 m12 m
3rd second10 m8 m
4th second10 m20 m

Examples: Uniform - ek khaali highway par cruise control se chalti gaadi, ghadi ki sui. Non-uniform - sheher ki traffic mein gaadi (rukti, chalti, tez hoti hai), girta hua patthar.

🚦 Asli Zindagi Mein Uniform Motion Bahut Kam!

Rozmarra ki zindagi mein bilkul uniform motion milna mushkil hai - traffic light, speed breaker, mod (turn) - sab speed badal dete hain. Isi liye physics mein hum aksar "average" nikalte hain, jo agla topic hai!

💨 Speed (Chaal)

📐 Speed = Distance ÷ Time
SI unit: metre per second (m/s). Ye ek scalar hai (direction nahi).

📌 Average Speed = Total distance ÷ Total time (jab speed badalti rehti ho).

🔄 Unit Conversion (Bahut Important!)

📐 km/h se m/s: × 5/18
m/s se km/h: × 18/5

✔ Conversion Examples:

72 km/h = 72 × 5/18 = 20 m/s
90 km/h = 90 × 5/18 = 25 m/s
20 m/s = 20 × 18/5 = 72 km/h

✔ Example 1 - Simple speed:

Ek car 240 km ki doori 4 ghante mein tay karti hai. Speed nikalo.
Speed = 240 ÷ 4 = 60 km/h

✔ Example 2 - Average speed:

Ek ladka pehle 100 m 20 second mein, phir agle 100 m 30 second mein chalta hai. Average speed?
Total distance = 100 + 100 = 200 m
Total time = 20 + 30 = 50 s
Average speed = 200 ÷ 50 = 4 m/s

Dhyan do: Ye 5 aur 3.33 ka simple average (4.17) NAHI hai! Hamesha total distance ÷ total time karo.

🧭 Velocity (Veg)

📐 Velocity = Displacement ÷ Time
SI unit: m/s. Ye ek vector hai (magnitude + direction).

Velocity batati hai ki object kis direction mein kitni tezi se ja raha hai. Isliye velocity zero ya negative bhi ho sakti hai - jab displacement zero ya ulti direction mein ho.

📌 Average velocity = Total displacement ÷ Total time
Aur jab acceleration uniform ho, tab: Average velocity = (u + v) ÷ 2
(u = initial velocity, v = final velocity)

✔ Example 3 - Round trip (velocity vs speed):

Ek aadmi ghar se 3 km door bazaar jaata hai aur wapas aa jaata hai. Poore safar mein 2 ghante lage.
Average speed = total distance ÷ time = (3+3) ÷ 2 = 3 km/h
Average velocity = total displacement ÷ time = 0 ÷ 2 = 0 (kyunki wapas wahin pahuncha!)

✔ Example 4 - Average velocity from u and v:

Ek gaadi ki velocity 10 m/s se badhkar 30 m/s ho jaati hai (uniform acceleration se). Average velocity?
= (u + v) ÷ 2 = (10 + 30) ÷ 2 = 20 m/s

🚗 Speedometer Velocity Nahi Batata!

Gaadi ka speedometer sirf speed batata hai (jaise 60 km/h) - wo direction nahi batata. Isi liye use "speed-o-meter" kehte hain, "velocity-meter" nahi! Direction jaanne ke liye compass ya GPS chahiye.

🏃 Ek Mazedaar Sawaal

Ek athlete 400 m ke gol track par ek poora chakkar 50 second mein pura karta hai.
▸ Average speed = 400 ÷ 50 = 8 m/s
▸ Average velocity = 0 ÷ 50 = 0!
Wo bhaag toh raha tha, lekin uski average velocity zero hai - kyunki wo wahin wapas pahuncha!
