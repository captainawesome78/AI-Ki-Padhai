Ek Parent, Bahut Bachche 🌟

Asexual reproduction mein ek hi parent bina kisi partner ke naye jeev bana leta hai - aur wo bachche parent ki bilkul copy (clone) hote hain. Ye tezi se hota hai. Chaliye iske paanch (5) main tareeke dekhte hain.

1️⃣ Fission (Tootna / Batwara)

Parent cell do (ya zyada) hisson mein baat jaata hai. Do type:

  • Binary fission - ek se DO. Example: Amoeba, Paramecium, bacteria.
  • Multiple fission - ek se BAHUT saare, ek saath. Example: Plasmodium (malaria wala parasite).
Amoeba binary fission: ek cell do barabar cells mein baant jaati hai.

2️⃣ Budding (Kali Nikalna)

Parent ke body par ek chhota "bud" (kali) nikalta hai, jo badhkar naya jeev ban jaata hai aur alag ho jaata hai. Example: Hydra aur Yeast.

3️⃣ Fragmentation (Tukdon Mein Toot-na)

Jeev ke body ke tukde ho jaate hain, aur har tukda ek naya jeev ban jaata hai. Example: Spirogyra (talaab wali hari algae).

4️⃣ Regeneration (Dobara Ugna)

Kuch jeev apne kate hue hisson se poora naya jeev bana lete hain. Agar Planaria (flatworm) ke tukde kar do, toh har tukde se poora naya Planaria ban jaata hai! Starfish apni kati hui baahu (arm) se poora naya body bana leti hai.

🦎 Chhipkali Ki Poonch

Chhipkali (lizard) apni tooti hui poonch (tail) wapas uga leti hai - ye regeneration ka ek roop hai! Lekin dhyan do: isse poora naya jeev nahi banta, sirf part wapas aata hai. Poora naya jeev banana (jaise Planaria) hi asli reproduction hai.

5️⃣ Spore Formation (Beej-numa Kann)

Kuch jeev chhote-chhote spores (dhool jaise kann) banate hain jo hawa mein udte hain aur nayi jagah girkar naye jeev bana dete hain. Example: Rhizopus (bread mould - bread par lagne wali kaali-hari fungus).

🍞 Ghar Par Dekho (dhyan se!)

Ek gili bread ko 2-3 din band dabbe mein rakho. Uspar kaali-safed roiyaan (mould) ug aayegi - ye Rhizopus hai! Iske upar wale chhote kaale dots hi spore-cases hain. (Ise chakhna ya kholkar sungh-na mat - fungus se allergy ho sakti hai. Dekhkar dabba band karke phenk do.)
