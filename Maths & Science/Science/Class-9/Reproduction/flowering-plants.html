Sexual Reproduction in Flowering Plants 🌺

Home Class 9 Science Reproduction Chapter

Phool = Plant Ka Reproductive Organ 🌸

Phool sirf khoobsurti ke liye nahi hote - ye plant ke reproductive organs hain! Phool ke andar hi male aur female parts hote hain jinse naya plant (seed ke roop mein) banta hai. Chaliye phool ke parts samjhein.

Ek phool ke main parts - male (stamen) aur female (carpel) dono ek saath.

🧩 Phool Ke 4 Main Parts

Protection

🍃 Sepals (Baahri hari pattiyan)

Phool ke sabse bahar, hare rang ke. Ye kali (bud) ko andar surakshit rakhte hain jab tak phool khilta nahi.

Attraction

🌸 Petals (Rangeen pankhudiyan)

Rangeen aur khushboo-daar. Ye keedon (insects), titliyon aur birds ko attract karte hain taaki wo pollination mein madad karein.

Male Part

♂️ Stamen (Pungkesar)

Phool ka male part. Do hisse: Anther (jo pollen grains - male gametes - banata hai) aur Filament (danthal jo anther ko pakadta hai).

Female Part

♀️ Carpel / Pistil (Stri-kesar)

Phool ka female part. Teen hisse: Stigma (upar wala chipchipa part jo pollen pakadta hai), Style (naali), aur Ovary (jisme ovules - female gametes/eggs - hote hain).

📌 Bisexual flower = jisme stamen aur carpel dono ho (jaise gudhal/hibiscus, gulab). Unisexual flower = jisme sirf ek ho (jaise papita, makka/corn).

🐝 Pollination (Paraag-sinchan)

Pollination = anther se pollen ka stigma tak pahunchna. Do type:

  • Self-pollination - pollen usi phool (ya usi plant) ke stigma par gire.
  • Cross-pollination - pollen doosre plant ke phool ke stigma par gire (isse variation aati hai).

Pollination ke agents (madadgaar):

  • 🐝 Biotic (zinda) - keede (bees), titliyan, birds, chamgadad.
  • 💨 Abiotic (na-zinda) - hawa (wind - jaise ghaas, makka), paani (water).
🐝 Madhumakkhi Ka Kaam

Jab madhumakkhi (honeybee) phool ka ras (nectar) peeti hai, toh uske body par pollen chipak jaata hai. Agle phool par jaakar wo pollen wahan gir jaata hai - aur pollination ho jaati hai! Isliye bees kheti ke liye bahut zaroori hain. Bina bees ke, bahut si fasal na ho.

💞 Fertilisation (Nivechan)

Pollination ke baad asli kaam shuru hota hai. Stigma par gira pollen grain ek pollen tube ugaata hai jo style ke through neeche ovary tak jaata hai. Pollen ka male gamete ovule ke andar egg (female gamete) se milta hai. Is milan ko Fertilisation kehte hain, aur isse banta hai Zygote (naye plant ki pehli cell).

Pollen tube style se neeche ovule tak - wahan male gamete egg se milta hai.

🍎 Seed aur Fruit Kaise Bante Hain?

Fertilisation ke baad phool ke parts badalne lagte hain:

Pehle (before)Baad mein (after fertilisation)
Ovule (beej-anda)→ Seed (beej)
Ovary→ Fruit (phal)
Zygote→ Embryo (baby plant seed ke andar)

Petals, sepals aur stamens sukhkar gir jaate hain - ab unka kaam khatam. Seed ke andar embryo aur khaana (food) hota hai, jo sahi jagah aur mausam milne par ugkar naya plant banata hai (germination).

🍅 Tamatar Ke Beej

Jab aap tamatar (tomato) ya seb (apple) kaatte ho aur andar beej dikhte hain - wo beej ovules the, aur jo gudda (fleshy part) khaate ho wo ovary tha jo fruit ban gaya! Ab har fruit ki kahani samajh aayegi.

🌬️ Seed Dispersal (Beej Ka Failaav)

Beej ek jagah gir jaayein toh sab plants ek doosre se compete karenge. Isliye nature beejon ko door-door bhejta hai:

  • 💨 Hawa (wind) - halke, par wale beej (jaise madar/aak, dandelion).
  • 💧 Paani (water) - tairne wale beej (jaise nariyal/coconut).
  • 🐾 Jaanwar (animals) - kaante wale beej jo baalon/kapdon se chipak jaate hain; ya fruits jo jaanwar khaate hain aur beej door girate hain.
  • 💥 Phatna (explosion) - kuch fali (pods) phatkar beej door phenkti hain (jaise matar/pea, castor).
