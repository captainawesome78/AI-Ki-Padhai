Ek Scientific & Natural Topic 🌱

Insaan sexual reproduction se naye jeev (baby) banate hain - do parents (mother aur father) ke milne se. Ye ek bilkul natural (prakritik) aur scientific process hai, jaise plants mein hota hai. Isse samajhna important hai taaki hum apni body ko jaanein aur health ka khayal rakhein. Iss lesson ko seedhe, scientific tarike se padho.

📌 Reproduction ke liye 3 cheezein chahiye: (1) male gamete = sperm, (2) female gamete = egg (ovum), aur (3) dono ka milna = fertilisation.

♂️ Male Reproductive System

Iska kaam: sperm (male gamete) banana aur pahunchana. Main parts:

Schematic - male system ka simple diagram.
  • Testes (do) - sperm banate hain, aur testosterone hormone bhi (jo puberty ke changes laata hai). Ye body ke bahar ek theli (scrotum) mein hote hain kyunki sperm banne ke liye body se thoda thanda taapmaan chahiye.
  • Sperm ducts (vas deferens) - naaliyan jo sperm ko aage le jaati hain.
  • Glands - ek fluid banate hain jisme sperm tairte hain (isse semen banta hai).

♀️ Female Reproductive System

Iska kaam: egg banana, fertilisation ki jagah dena, aur baby ko badhaana. Main parts:

Schematic - female system ka simple diagram.
  • Ovaries (do) - eggs (ova) banate hain aur female hormones (estrogen). Ladki janm se hi hazaaron eggs ke saath paida hoti hai.
  • Fallopian tubes (oviducts) - egg ovary se uterus tak inhi se jaata hai. Yahin fertilisation hoti hai.
  • Uterus (garbhashay/womb) - yahan fertilised egg se baby 9 mahine tak badhta hai.
  • Vagina - uterus ko bahar se jodne wala rasta; delivery ke waqt baby yahin se bahar aata hai.

💞 Fertilisation aur Pregnancy

Jab sperm aur egg fallopian tube mein milte hain, toh fertilisation hoti hai aur banta hai Zygote (ek single cell - naye insaan ki shuruaat). Fir:

Zygote baar-baar divide hokar embryo banta hai, jo uterus ki deewar se chipak jaata hai. Dheere-dheere ye foetus (jisme body parts ban jaate hain) banta hai. Maa ke sharir se baby ko khaana aur oxygen placenta ke through milta hai. Takreeban 9 mahine (38-40 hafte) baad baby ka janm (birth) hota hai.

🗓️ Menstrual Cycle (Maasik Dharm)

Har mahine female body ek baby ke liye taiyaari karti hai: ovary se ek egg release hota hai (ovulation), aur uterus ki deewar mote (thick) & naram ho jaati hai taaki fertilised egg wahan reh sake.

  • Agar egg fertilise ho jaaye - pregnancy shuru ho jaati hai.
  • Agar egg fertilise na ho - uterus ki extra deewar (khoon + tissue) toot kar body se bahar aa jaati hai. Isse menstruation (period / maahwaari) kehte hain.

Ye cycle takreeban 28 din ka hota hai aur usually 10-15 saal ki umar mein shuru hota hai. Ye ek bilkul normal aur healthy process hai - iski wajah se hi aage chalkar reproduction mumkin hai.

💚 Yaad rakho: Menstruation koi bimari ya "gandi" cheez nahi - ye ek natural body process hai jise har ladki anubhav karti hai. Iske baare mein khulkar, izzat ke saath baat karni chahiye. Hygiene (saaf-safai) ka dhyan rakho aur agar dard ya pareshaani ho toh bade / doctor se baat karo.
