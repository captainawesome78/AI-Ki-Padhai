Hello Students! 👋

Har jeev ek din boodha hokar mar jaata hai. Phir bhi duniya jeevon se bhari hui hai - insaan, ped, jaanwar, keede. Aisa kaise? Kyunki har jeev apne jaise naye jeev banata hai. Isi process ko Reproduction (prajanan) kehte hain. Ye zindagi ko aage badhaata hai - ek peedhi (generation) se agli peedhi tak.

📌 Definition: Reproduction wo process hai jisme jeev apne jaise naye individuals (offspring) paida karte hain, taaki unki species (jaati) zinda rahe.

🤔 Reproduction Zaroori Kyun Hai?

  • Species zinda rahe: Agar reproduction na ho, toh koi bhi species dheere-dheere khatam (extinct) ho jaayegi - jaise dinosaur.
  • Variation (badlaav): Reproduction, khaas kar sexual reproduction, chhote-chhote differences (variations) paida karta hai. Yahi variations evolution aur survival ki neev hain.
🧬 DNA - The "Blueprint"

Reproduction mein parent apne DNA (body banane ka "instruction manual") offspring ko deta hai. Isi wajah se bachche apne parents jaise dikhte hain. Lekin DNA hu-ba-hu copy nahi hota, isliye har bachcha thoda alag (unique) hota hai.

🌿 vs 👫 Do Main Tareeke

Reproduction do tarah ka hota hai:

Asexual = ek parent, same copies. Sexual = do parents, naya unique offspring.

📊 Asexual vs Sexual - Comparison

BaatAsexual ReproductionSexual Reproduction
Kitne parents?Sirf 1 (single parent)2 (male + female)
Gametes (sperm/egg)?Nahi banteHaan, bante hain
Offspring kaisa?Parent ki hu-ba-hu copy (clone)Parents se alag (variation)
SpeedTez (fast)Dheera (slow)
Variation?Na ke barabarBahut (evolution ke liye accha)
ExamplesBacteria, Amoeba, Hydra, YeastInsaan, jaanwar, phool wale plants

📌 Ek line mein: Asexual = "ek parent, fast, same copies". Sexual = "do parents, slow, variety ke saath".

🧠 Chalo Test Karein! (Quick Quiz)

Apne aap ko check karo 👇

Q1. Asexual reproduction mein kitne parents hote hain?
Q2. Variation (badlaav) sabse zyada kisme hota hai?
Q3. Parent se offspring mein kya transfer hota hai (jisse bachche parents jaise dikhte hain)?
