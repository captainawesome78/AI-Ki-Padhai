Vegetative Propagation 🌱

Bina Beej ke Naya Plant! 🌿

Kya aapne dekha hai ki gulab (rose) ki ek tehni (branch) mitti mein lagao toh naya poora paudha ug jaata hai? Ye Vegetative Propagation hai - jisme plant ke vegetative parts (root, stem, ya leaf) se naya plant banta hai, bina beej (seed) ke. Ye asexual reproduction ka hi ek roop hai, isliye naya plant parent ki bilkul copy hota hai.

🌿 Natural (Prakritik) Tareeke

Kudrat khud kai plants ko aise ugaati hai:

PartKaiseExample
Stem (tuber)Aloo ki "aankhein" (eyes) se naye plantAloo (Potato)
Stem (rhizome)Zameen ke andar tanaAdrak (Ginger), Haldi

LeafPatti ke kinaron par chhoti kaliyan (buds)Bryophyllum (Patharchatta)
RootJadon se naye shootsShakarkand (Sweet potato)
🥔 Aloo Ki "Aankhein"

Aloo par jo chhote gadde (dents) hote hain, unhe "eyes" (aankhein) kehte hain. Har aankh ek chhoti stem-bud hai. Isi wajah se ghar mein rakha aloo kabhi-kabhi ankur (sprout) nikaal deta hai! Yahi aankhein bo do toh naye aloo ke plant ug aate hain.

✂️ Artificial (Manav-nirmit) Tareeke

Kisan aur maali (gardeners) in tareekon se plants ugaate hain:

  • Cutting - stem ka tukda mitti mein lagao. Example: gulab, ganna, money plant.
  • Layering - branch ko jhukakar mitti se dabao, jad nikalne par kaat do. Example: chameli (jasmine), nimbu.
  • Grafting - do plants ke parts jodkar ek banao (ek ki jad, doosre ki tehni). Example: aam (mango), seb (apple).
  • Tissue culture - lab mein ek chhote tissue se hazaaron plants. Example: orchid, banana.

✅ Faayde (Advantages)

  • Tez: Beej se plant ugne mein time lagta hai; vegetative se jaldi.
  • Same quality: Naya plant parent ki copy hota hai - agar parent meethe aam deta hai, toh naya bhi meethe aam dega.
  • Bina-beej wale plants: Kela (banana), santra (orange), gulab - inme achhe beej nahi bante, isliye vegetative propagation se hi ugaaye jaate hain.
🇮🇳 Kheti Mein Kaam

Bharat mein ganna (sugarcane), aloo, kela, aur aam ki kheti bade paimane par vegetative propagation se hoti hai. Isse kisan ko har baar ek jaisi, achhi quality ki fasal milti hai.
