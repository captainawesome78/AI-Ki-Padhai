Animal Tissues Ki Duniya 🐾

Plant tissues ke baad, ab animal (aur insaan) ke tissues. Animals move karte hain, sochte hain, khaate hain - isliye unke tissues bhi zyada tarah ke hote hain. Animal tissues 4 main types ke hote hain:

📌 4 Animal Tissues: (1) Epithelial - covering/lining, (2) Connective - jodne wale, (3) Muscular - movement, (4) Nervous - message. Is lesson mein pehle 2, agle lesson mein baaki 2.

🧱 Epithelial Tissue — Body Ki "Packaging"

🎁 Analogy: Wrapper / Raincoat

Socho epithelial tissue body ka "wrapper" ya "raincoat" hai. Ye body ke bahar (skin) aur andar (mouth, stomach, blood vessels) har surface ko cover aur line karta hai. Ye ek guard ki tarah kaam karta hai - bahar ki cheezon ko rokta hai aur andar ko protect karta hai.

Epithelial cells ek doosre se chipke (tightly packed) hote hain, bina kisi gap ke - taaki koi cheez beech se na nikal sake. Iske kaam: protection, absorption (soakna), aur secretion (banakar chhodna).

Epithelial cells apni shape ke hisaab se alag types ke hote hain.

Types of Epithelium

  • Squamous (chapte, patle cells) - skin ki upar wali layer, mooh aur blood vessels ki lining. Protection deta hai.
  • Cuboidal (cube jaise) - kidney tubules aur glands mein. Absorption & secretion.
  • Columnar (lambe pillars) - stomach aur intestine ki lining. Khaana absorb karta hai.
  • Ciliated columnar - ispar chhote baal (cilia) hote hain jo mucus aur dhool ko push karte hain (saans ki naali / respiratory tract mein).
  • Glandular - ye "glands" banata hai jo pasina, saliva, hormones secrete karte hain.
🤧 Naak Mein Cilia

Aapki naak aur windpipe mein ciliated epithelium hota hai. Ye chhote baal (cilia) lagataar hilte hain aur dhool, dhuaan aur germs ko bahar dhakelte hain - taaki wo aapke fephdon (lungs) tak na pahunche. Ek natural air-filter!

🔗 Connective Tissue — Body Ka "Glue & Packing"

🧶 Analogy: Jodne aur Bharne Wala

Connective tissue body ke alag-alag parts ko jodta, support karta aur bharta hai - jaise ghar mein cement, taar aur packing material. Iski khaas baat: cells door-door hote hain aur beech mein ek "matrix" (jelly, fluid ya solid padarth) bhara hota hai.

Matrix ke hisaab se connective tissue ke types:

Fluid matrix

🩸 Blood (Khoon)

Haan, blood bhi ek connective tissue hai! Iska matrix liquid (plasma) hai jisme RBC (oxygen le jaate), WBC (germs se ladte), aur platelets (chot par khoon jamate) tairte hain. Blood poore body mein oxygen, khana aur waste transport karta hai.

Hard matrix

🦴 Bone (Haddi)

Iska matrix hard (calcium aur phosphorus) hota hai. Ye body ka framework (dhaancha) banata hai, organs ko protect karta hai (jaise skull dimaag ko), aur muscles ko attach hone ki jagah deta hai.

Flexible

👂 Cartilage (Upsthi)

Smooth aur flexible tissue. Aapki naak ki nok aur kaan cartilage se bane hain (isi liye mud jaate hain par tootte nahi). Ye joints mein bhi hoti hai taaki haddiyan aapas mein ragad na khaayein.

Jodne wale

🔗 Tendon & Ligament

Tendon = muscle ko bone se jodta hai (strong, kam lachdaar).
Ligament = bone ko bone se jodta hai (strong aur elastic/lachdaar). Jab log "ligament tear" ki baat karte hain (khiladiyon mein), toh yahi tissue phatta hai.

Bharne wale

🧈 Areolar & Adipose

Areolar tissue organs ke beech ki khaali jagah bharta hai aur support deta hai.
Adipose tissue fat (charbi) store karta hai - skin ke neeche. Ye body ko garam rakhta hai (insulation) aur energy ka store hai.

🐫 Adipose Tissue Ka Kamaal

Oont (camel) ke hump (kubbad) mein aur seals/whales ki mothi khaal mein adipose (fat) tissue hota hai jo unhe mushkil waqt mein energy aur garmi deta hai. Hamari body bhi extra energy fat ke roop mein store karti hai.
