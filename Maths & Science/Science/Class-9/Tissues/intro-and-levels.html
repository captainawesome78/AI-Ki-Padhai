Hello Students! 👋

Pichle chapter mein humne jaana ki har jeev cells se bana hai. Lekin ek sawaal socho — aapke body mein crores cells hain, kya sabhi ek jaisa kaam karte hain? Nahi! Kuch cells sochne ka kaam karte hain (brain), kuch khoon bahane ka (blood), kuch chalne ka (muscles). Jab ek jaise cells milkar ek khaas kaam karte hain, toh unke group ko Tissue kehte hain.

🏏 Sabse Aasan Analogy — Cricket Team

🏏 Division of Labour

Ek cricket team socho. Sabhi 11 players insaan hain, lekin har koi ek jaisa kaam nahi karta — batsman run banata hai, bowler gend daalta hai, wicket-keeper catch pakadta hai. Har group apne kaam mein expert hai. Isse team achha khelti hai.

Body mein bhi yahi hota hai! Cells ne aapas mein kaam baant liya hai — ise "Division of Labour" kehte hain. Ek jaise kaam wale cells ek team (tissue) banate hain. Isi wajah se hamari body itni achhe se kaam karti hai.

📌 Definition: Tissue ek aise cells ka group hai jo (1) structure mein ek jaise hote hain, (2) ek hi jagah se aate hain (same origin), aur (3) milkar ek khaas kaam (function) karte hain.

🦠 Unicellular vs Multicellular

Amoeba jaise unicellular (ek cell wale) jeev mein sirf ek hi cell hoti hai, jo saare kaam khud karti hai — khaana, saans, movement, sab kuch. Usse tissues ki zaroorat nahi.

Lekin insaan, ped, jaanwar jaise multicellular (bahut cell wale) jeevon mein cells ne kaam baant liya, isliye unhe tissues chahiye. Jitna bada aur complex jeev, utne organised tissues.

🤔 Kya Aap Jaante Hain?

Amoeba ka ek hi cell chalne, khaane aur reproduce karne — sab kuch karta hai. Lekin iska "size" nahi badh sakta zyada, kyunki ek akeli cell itna kaam sambhaal nahi sakti. Isliye bade jeevon ne "teamwork" (tissues) apnaya.

🪜 Levels of Organisation

Body ek building ki tarah "step-by-step" bani hoti hai — chhote se bade tak. Ise levels of organisation kehte hain:

Cell se organism tak — har level pichle level se milkar banta hai.

✔ Ek asli example (dil / heart):

Muscle cells → milkar banate hain muscle tissue → milkar banata hai heart (organ) → blood vessels ke saath milkar banata hai circulatory system → aur poori body milkar banti hai ek organism (insaan).

🌱 vs 🐾 Plant aur Animal Tissues — Ek Jhalak

Plants aur animals dono mein tissues hote hain, lekin unki zaroorat alag hai:

BaatPlant Tissues 🌱Animal Tissues 🐾
MovementPlant ek jagah khada rehta hai (stationary)Animal idhar-udhar chalta hai
GrowthSirf khaas jagah par (tips) - meristemPoore body mein barabar
Support ke liyeBahut saare dead (mari hui) cells - kyunki support chahiye, energy kamZyada-tar living cells
Khaas tissueSupport wale tissues zyadaMuscle aur nerve tissue (movement + sochne ke liye)
