Plant Ki "Growth Factory" 🏭

Kabhi socha hai ki ped kaise badhta hai? Aap toh poore body se badhte ho, lekin plant sirf kuch khaas jagahon se badhta hai. In khaas jagahon par ek special tissue hota hai jiske cells hamesha divide hote rehte hain — ise Meristematic Tissue (meristem) kehte hain. Word "meristem" Greek word "merizein" se aaya hai, jiska matlab hai "divide karna".

👶 Analogy: Baby Cells Ki Factory

Meristem ko ek factory samjho jahan lagataar naye "baby cells" bante rehte hain. Ye baby cells pehle divide hote hain, phir bade hokar (aur badalkar) plant ke doosre tissues ban jaate hain. Yaani har naya tissue meristem se hi shuru hota hai!

🔎 Meristem Cells Ki Pehchaan

Meristem cells ki kuch khaas nishaniyan hain (kyunki inhe fatafat divide karna hota hai):

  • Cells chhote aur ek jaise (compact) hote hain.
  • Patli cell wall (thin wall) hoti hai.
  • Dense cytoplasm aur bada nucleus hota hai.
  • Vacuole bahut chhota ya na ke barabar (kyunki space chahiye kaam ke liye).
  • Cells ke beech koi khaali jagah (intercellular space) nahi hoti.

📌 Yaad rakho: Meristem cells "young aur active" hote hain — thin wall, big nucleus, no vacuole, always dividing. Ye living cells hain.

📍 3 Types (Location Ke Hisaab Se)

Teen jagah meristem hota hai: tips (apical), sides (lateral), aur leaf-base (intercalary).
Tips par

1. Apical Meristem

Root aur shoot ke sabse aage wale hisse (tips) par. Ye plant ki lambai (length/height) badhata hai. Isi wajah se ped uupar ki taraf aur jadein neeche ki taraf badhti hain. Ise primary growth kehte hain.

Side par

2. Lateral Meristem (Cambium)

Stem aur root ke side (baglon) mein. Ye plant ki motai/chaudai (girth/thickness) badhata hai. Isi wajah se ped ka tana (trunk) mota hota jaata hai. Ise secondary growth kehte hain.

Beech mein

3. Intercalary Meristem

Pattiyon (leaves) ke base par ya nodes ke beech mein. Ye ghaas (grass) aur ganne (sugarcane) jaise plants mein hota hai. Isi wajah se ghaas kaatne ke baad phir se ug aati hai!

🌿 Real-Life Connection

⚽ Cricket Ground & Lawn

Aapne dekha hoga ki cricket ground ya ghar ke lawn ki ghaas baar-baar kaatne par bhi phir ug aati hai. Kyun? Kyunki ghaas mein intercalary meristem base par hota hai — upar se kaato, neeche se wo dobara badha deta hai! Agar growth sirf tip (apical) par hoti, toh kaatne ke baad ghaas kabhi na badhti.

🌳 Ped Ke "Rings"

Ped ka tana kaatne par jo gol-gol rings (chhalle) dikhte hain, wo lateral meristem (cambium) ki wajah se bante hain — har saal ek naya ring. Rings gin kar ped ki umar (age) pata ki ja sakti hai!
