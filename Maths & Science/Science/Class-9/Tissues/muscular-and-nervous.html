Action Aur Message 🎬

Ab do sabse mazedaar animal tissues. Muscular tissue body ko hilata-dulata hai (movement), aur Nervous tissue poori body mein bijli ki speed se messages bhejta hai. In dono ki wajah se aap chal-fir sakte ho, mehsoos kar sakte ho, aur react kar sakte ho.

💪 Muscular Tissue — Movement Machine

Muscle cells lambe hote hain, isliye inhe muscle fibres kehte hain. Ye sikudte (contract) aur phailte (relax) hain - isi se movement hoti hai. Teen types hain:

Teen tarah ke muscles - har ek alag jagah, alag kaam.
Voluntary (control mein)

1. Striated / Skeletal Muscle

Ye haddiyon se judi hoti hai (isliye "skeletal"). Ispar stripes (dhaariyan) hoti hain (isliye "striated"). Ye hamare control (voluntary) mein hai - jaise haath uthana, daudna. Cells lambe, cylindrical aur multinucleate (kai nucleus) hote hain. Ye thak (tire) jaate hain, isliye aaram chahiye.

Involuntary (auto)

2. Smooth / Unstriated Muscle

Bina stripes wali, spindle (dono taraf nukeeli) shape, ek nucleus. Ye hamare control ke bina (involuntary) kaam karti hai. Stomach, aant (intestine), aur blood vessels ki deewaron mein hoti hai - khaana aage badhati hai bina hamare soche.

Never stops!

3. Cardiac Muscle

Ye sirf dil (heart) mein hoti hai. Branched (shaakhaon wali), striped, aur involuntary. Iski sabse kamaal baat: ye zindagi bhar bina ruke, bina thake dhadakti rehti hai! Din-raat, sote-jaagte.

❤️ Dil Kabhi Nahi Thakta

Aapka dil ek din mein takreeban 1,00,000 baar dhadakta hai - aur zindagi bhar bina ruke! Ye cardiac muscle ka kamaal hai. Agar ye skeletal muscle hoti (jo thak jaati), toh hum zinda hi nahi reh paate.

🧠 Nervous Tissue — Body Ka "Message System"

⚡ Analogy: Bijli Ke Taar

Nervous tissue body ke "electric wires" hain. Jab aap garam cheez chhoote ho, ek message bijli ki speed se aapke haath se dimaag tak jaata hai aur turant wapas - "haath hatao!". Ye sab nervous tissue karta hai. Iski cell ko Neuron kehte hain.

Ek neuron: dendrites message lete hain, axon door tak pahunchata hai.

Neuron ke 3 parts:

  • Dendrites - chhoti shakhaayein jo message receive karti hain.
  • Cell body - jisme nucleus hota hai.
  • Axon - lamba "taar" jo message door tak bhejta hai.
📏 Sabse Lamba Cell

Neuron humari body ka sabse lamba cell ho sakta hai - kuch neurons 1 meter se bhi lambe hote hain (spinal cord se paer tak)! Aur message ki speed 100+ meter/second tak ho sakti hai. Isi liye aap itni jaldi react kar paate ho.
