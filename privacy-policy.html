Privacy Policy for AIkiPadhai

Last Updated: November 2025

AIkiPadhai ("hum", "hamara", "website") par aapki privacy hamare liye important hai. Yeh policy batati hai ki hum kaunsi information collect karte hain aur use kaise use karte hain.

Information We Collect

Hum direct user information collect nahi karte, jaise ki naam ya email, jab tak aap humein contact form ya email ke through khud provide na karein.

  • Direct Information: Hum aapse direct personal information (jaise naam ya email) tabhi collect karte hain jab aap humein contact form ya email ke zariye khud provide karte hain.
  • Indirect Information: Jab aap hamari website visit karte hain, hamara server automatically kuch non-personal information record kar sakta hai, jaise aapka IP address, browser type, aur visit ka time. Yeh standard web practice hai.

Third-Party Services & Cookies

Hum apni services ko operate karne ke liye third-party vendors ka use karte hain:

  • Google AdSense: Hum apni website par ads display karne ke liye Google AdSense ka use karte hain. Google, ek third-party vendor ke roop me, ads serve karne ke liye cookies ka use karta hai. Google ka DART cookie ka use aapki site aur internet par anya sites par aapke visits ke aadhar par ads serve karne me saksham banata hai. Aap Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy par jaakar DART cookie ke use se opt-out kar sakte hain.
  • Google AdSense: Hum apni website par ads display karne ke liye Google AdSense ka use karte hain. Google, ek third-party vendor ke roop me, ads serve karne ke liye cookies ka istemal karta hai. Google ka DART cookie ka use aapki site aur internet par anya sites par aapke visits ke aadhar par ads serve karne me saksham banata hai. Aap Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy par jaakar DART cookie ke use se opt-out kar sakte hain.

How We Use Information

Jo bhi non-personal information (jaise browser type, visit duration) collect hoti hai, woh sirf website traffic analyze karne aur user experience improve karne ke liye use hoti hai.

Collect ki gayi information ka use in maqsadon ke liye hota hai:

  • Aapke sawalon ka jawab dene ke liye.
  • Website par relevant ads dikhane ke liye.
  • Website traffic analyze karke user experience ko behtar banane ke liye.

Data Ki Suraksha (Data Security)

Hum aapki information ki suraksha ke liye reasonable technical measures lete hain. Lekin, internet par koi bhi method 100% secure nahi hai.

Aapke Adhikar (Your Rights)

Aap humse contact karke yeh jaan sakte hain ki hamare paas aapki kaunsi personal information hai aur use delete karne ki request kar sakte hain.

Contact Us
